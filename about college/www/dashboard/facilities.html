Library

The Library is one of the oldest libraries
in Madurai and houses a rare and valuable
collection of books. The general library
and department libraries have a total of
89,190 books on various subjects. The
library has a rare collection of ancient
books both in Tamil and English languages.
The periodical section has back-volumes of
Journals in all disciplines.
The library has bought 76 Journals.

Hostels

Hostels are available for both boys and girls.
Available facilities in Boys hostel:
Library, Audio Visual Aids, Invertors, Health
Centre,Shuttle Cock Play Ground,Recreation
Club, Various Student Committees for Mess,
Library, Sports and Water
Management have been formed
and are functioning well.
Available facilities in Women’s hostel:
Internet facilities, Coffee Vending Machine,
Audio Visual Aids and Invertors

Post Office

The post office is situated adjacent to the
canteen. It provides the students a
facility to write letters to their parents,
though writing letters has become an
outdated art. Students send various forms
of applications to different places from
within the campus.

Canteen

The canteen provides the staff and
the students a means for quenching
their thirst and feeding their hungry
stomachs in the midst of their busy
academic schedule. It remains a much-
sought place and always reminds one of
the couplet in the Thirukkural,
“One will be provided with food
for stomach, when there is no food for ear”.

Placement Cell

The placement cell was established in
2003 with an intention to inculcate a
sense of responsibility among students to
be placed in higher positions. The cell
functions as a vital organ to bridge the
gap between companies and students
with a focus on employability.

Career Guidance Cell

The Career Guidance Cell was started
in 2003. The cell prepare the students
for campus interviews and job placement
and train the students for competitive
and civil service exams. It arranges
training programme on soft skill
development and provide career counseling.

Women's Counseling Cell

Girls Students' Counselling Cell takes
care of the well being, welfare and
personal development of the girls.
It works towards developing their
personality and self-esteem. Our on-site
counselling system provides support to
the girls and enables them to
understand and resolve their problems.

First Aid Cell

The college runs a First Aid Cell
to facilitate immediate medical assistance
to students and staff. A first
aid box with all necessary medicines
and dressing materials is maintained. The
cell offers a separate paper on First Aid to
Youth Red Cross Society volunteers of the
college under Part V course. Besides theory,
practical training is given to
students by St.John’s Ambulance.
