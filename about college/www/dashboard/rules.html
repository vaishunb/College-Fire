Common Rules


Students should dress appropriately.
Cell phones are not allowed inside the campus.
Students should help keep the college campus clean.
Students should not smoke inside the campus.
Students should wear their identity cards at all times.
Ragging is strictly prohibited.
Students are encouraged to regularly check the
notice board for current information.
No documents or promotional materials should be
circulated without the permission of the Principal.
Students must respect college premises, buildings and
furniture.
Students who disobey the college rules will be taken to task.
